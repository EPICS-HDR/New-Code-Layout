Welcome, this website is dedicated to compiling, displaying, and interpreting numerous varieties of environmental data for Standing Rock Nation. The website pulls data from 6 dams, 13 gauges and 4 mesonets in the area and displays them in several different ways. This site incorporates an interactive map where you can select any of the types of data and view monthly graphs. There is also a custom graph option where you can graph any data type with data from years past.
